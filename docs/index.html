Homo Antecessor

Stone Tool Use

The earliest stone tools are catergorized as "Oldowan." They have been connected to Homo Antecessor (who are the earliest hominin species discovered in Europe). They were capable of simple stone tool production.

Hunting

Evidence for hunting has been found from percussive marks on animal bones.
Evidence of Burials
There is no evidence of proper burials within Homo Antecessor. It is thought that these hominins practiced canabilism.
